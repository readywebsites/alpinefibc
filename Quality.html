Quality

Quality of our products and Service satisfaction of our customer is our prime focus.

Alpine FIBC maintain quality right from the selection of raw material to the final delivery. The Quality Assurance team ensures that products meet all the required quality standards at each and every stage.

We focus on hygiene and cleanliness to ensure Food and Pharma grade FIBCs that are sanitized and free from dust, germs, and pollutants.

At different levels of the manufacturing process, our in-house “Quality Assurance System” strictly follows:

  • ✔ Raw material testing – Certified by Major raw material manufacturers
  • ✔ Tape – Yarn width, denier of the yarn, strength and elongation
  • ✔ Fabric – Size, mesh, strength, elongation, GSM weight
  • ✔ Cutting – Weaving defects, size of cutting
  • ✔ Stitching – Seam strength, bag weight, mass, cleaning, and printing
  • ✔ Packing – Compacting and proper bailing

We fulfill the specific requirements of customers through advanced machinery, a fully equipped quality control laboratory, highly experienced staff, and approved superior food-grade polymers and additives.
