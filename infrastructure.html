Infrastructure

  • Extrusion: 2 machines with 600 kg/hr capacity for polypropylene tapes.
  • Looms: 49 circular looms, including 2 for ventilated fabric.
  • Belt Weaving: 10 needle looms & 1 dustproof machine.
  • Lamination: 90 mm plant with 2.05 m width capacity.
  • Fabrilator: PP thread production for food-grade bags.
  • Fabric Cutting: 3 jumbo fabric cutting lines (60″ & 80″).
  • Belt Cutting: 3 automatic belt/webbing cutting machines.
  • Printing: 2 jumbo bag printers (4-color) + roll-to-roll printer.
  • Auto Cutting: 2 auto cutting and stitching machines.
  • Liner Plants: 3 multi-layer extrusion plants for liners.
  • Gluing Machine: For glue-fitted liners.
  • Metal Detector: For food-grade FIBC bags.
  • Multi Filament Plant: Capacity from 650 to 4000 Denier.

Clean Room Facility: We produce FIBCs from a state-of-the-art clean room facility to meet strict international standards (ISO 9001, ISO 14000, ISO 22000). The clean room includes ultrasonic cutting, air washing, metal detecting, and follows HACCP for food safety.

Testing Lab: Equipped to perform breaking strength tests, UV tests, elongation, dart impact, coefficient of friction, air permeability, conductivity, and UN tests per Indian and international standards.
